Dr. Arturo Molina

Headmaster

Contact

Arturo Molina was born in Oaxaca, Mexico on February 1st, 1964. He received the B.S. degree and master on Computational Systems from Tecnologico de Monterrey, his Ph. D. on Mechanics from Budapest University of Technology and Economy and his Ph. D. degree on Manufacturing Systems from Loughborough University, United Kingdom. He is Vice-President of Research at the Tecnologico ́ de Monterrey.

He is member of the National Systems of Researchers of Mexico on Level III. He has published four books, 43 articles on arbitrated journals, 58 book chapters and 60 articles con arbitrated conferences. His areas of investigation include concurrent engineering, information models for design and manufacturing, technology modeling and integration in manufacturing and technologies for collaborative engineering.

Dr. Gerardo Carrera

Collaborator

Contact

Gerardo Carrera Mendoza received the B.S. degree Computer engineering and his master in science and computer engineering with honorific mention from Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México, in 2004 and 2007, respectively. He received his Ph.D. in computer science from Universidad de Imperial College, United Kingdom, on january 2012.

On 2012, he worked as Subdirector in risk methodologies in Comisió Nacional Bancaria y de Valores. Later, from 2012 to 2014 he was director of software development in División Científica de la Policia Federal (now part of Secretaría de Gobernación).

From 2014 to 2015 he worked in Instituto Federal de Telecomunicaciones, first as director of stadistics, later as director of technical analysis. From 2015 to 2017 he worked as Director of IT of LUCA Desarrollos Integrales en Tecnología S.A de C.V.

Since july 2015 to date, he is professor of Instituto Tecnológico de Estudios Superiores de Monterrey (ITESM). His academic and investigation interests are: Machine learning, computer vision and data analysis.

Post PhD in autonomous vehicles in the Berlin Public University with PhD.
