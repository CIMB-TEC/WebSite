The Center for Research in Microsystems and Biodesign (CIMB) is an initiative generated by a group of professors and students of the Tecnológico de Monterrey in Campus Mexico City since 2009.

Our group seeks to generate quality projects to improve the quality of life of Mexican society. Our center works through the generation of Postgraduate and Undergraduate projects on different lines of research.

We are a multidisciplinary group that combine their knowledge in the areas of Microsystems, Biodesign, Telecommunications, Biotechnology, Mechatronics, Artificial Intelligence, Exoskeletons, Biomedical, Autonomous Vehicles, Domotics and others.

Currently we develop the following projects:

  • Emotional Domotics
  • Advanced monitoring and driver assistance systems (ADMAS)
  • Exoesqueleton (ExoDUX)
  • Acoustic agriculture

Any additional information contact us.
